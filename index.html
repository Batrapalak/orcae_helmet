About Our HAWK HELMET HH1100

Providing solutions for military aviation At Orcas , we are commited to develop & deliver high quality ultra-lightweight helmet units for military aircrew to boost their confidence in toughest times. Our foray is in development of helmets with (DEBEL) under Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO)

Our helmet is equipped with a dual Polycarbonate visor system with independent locking of visors, user adjustable comfort padding and fitting system, mask attachment assembly, chin strap assembly, adjustable Ear Cup assembly and aircraft compatible communication assembly. At our research facilities in Manesar , equipped with in house ballistic tooling we ensure that our quality surpasses industry standards while providing cost effective solutions . From concept to production , we have capable infrastructure for all. Further we are explanding our range of products in defence, and enhance our expertise in these areas.

Our HAWK HELMET HH1100

The helmet has a rigid outer shell to accommodate the Visor assembly, Ear cup assembly, Chinstrap assembly, Oxygen mask attachment assembly, and the Communication assembly
The energy absorbing liner is fitted inside rigid shell. A comfort liner / adjustment pads covers the energy absorbing liner.
Edge rolls, fitted all along the periphery of the Helmet, provide good seal around the contour of the face.
The comfort liner and Edge rolls are fitted using a Velcro Hook-Loop arrangement. Additionally the Edge rolls are secured to the helmet using elastic loops.
The Helmet is equipped with a Nape Pad support, which can be adjusted as desired to suit individual fitment.
